## The Challenge

Ontarians who qualified for provincial benefits were failing to claim them — not due to ineligibility, but due to the complexity and inaccessibility of the discovery and application process.

## Our Approach

We engaged directly with the communities most affected, conducting research in partnership with community organizations to understand the real barriers: literacy, digital access, distrust of government systems, and language.

## Outcomes

- Measurable increase in successful benefit applications in pilot regions
- Reduction in application support calls
- Improved accessibility scores across the platform
