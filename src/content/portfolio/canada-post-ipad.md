*Role: Senior Information Architect & UX Lead (tablet experience) — Canada Post Corporation.*

## Overview

Part of modernizing Canada Post's fragmented mobile ecosystem, this project delivered the **iPad** experience — using the larger canvas for multi-panel layouts that let users track parcels, find post offices, manage deliveries, and access e-commerce tools without losing context.

## The Challenge

- Consistency across multiple device platforms and form factors
- Optimizing touch interactions for the tablet canvas
- Meeting WCAG accessibility and government digital standards
- Balancing complex logistics data with consumer usability

## Our Approach

- Stakeholder and user research; benchmarking against competitive delivery apps
- Hierarchical navigation and task-optimized flows in Axure and Sketch
- Multi-panel tablet layouts enabling parallel workflows
- Modern UI principles with real-time tracking visuals
- Accessibility testing across diverse user groups, with cross-platform guidelines documented

## Outcomes

- Launched a fully redesigned tablet experience with strong platform consistency
- Higher user-satisfaction scores and app-store ratings
- Reduced parcel-tracking time by over **30%** through simplified task flows
- Established an internal model for accessible, platform-consistent design
