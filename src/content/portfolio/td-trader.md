*Role: Senior User Experience Designer — TD Bank, Direct Investing / Wealth Management.*

## Overview

This initiative modernized TD's online trading platform into a high-performance, intuitive, and accessible experience for both novice and advanced investors — improving market-data analysis, trade execution, and a personalized dashboard, all aligned to enterprise design standards.

## The Challenge

- Dense information layouts lacking visual hierarchy
- Disjointed navigation that forced context-switching across tools
- Insufficient mobile responsiveness
- WCAG 2.1 / AODA accessibility gaps
- Design language inconsistent with TD's broader system

## Our Approach

- Discovery through user interviews and contextual research across investor personas
- A modular dashboard architecture enabling customization
- Three-zone navigation — **Research, Trade, Portfolio** — to reduce cognitive load
- Progressive-disclosure patterns for analytics
- Responsive prototypes in Figma and Axure, with WCAG 2.1 AA implementation
- Usability testing and eye-tracking studies

## Outcomes

- **25%** reduction in average trade-execution time
- **30%+** increase in active-user engagement
- Full WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- Higher satisfaction around usability and data clarity
- Reusable financial UI components integrated into other TD products
