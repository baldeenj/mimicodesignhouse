*Role: Senior / Lead UX Designer — RBC Royal Bank, Commercial & Corporate Banking.*

## Overview

RBC Express serves commercial and corporate clients managing cash flow, payments, and treasury operations. This initiative transformed the platform's entry point from a static, data-heavy landing page into a personalized, responsive dashboard delivering real-time financial insights and operational control across devices — from desktop down to mobile web.

## The Challenge

- Dense, text-based information that required extensive navigation to reach
- Minimal personalization or prioritization of the data that mattered most
- Inconsistent design patterns between web and tablet
- WCAG 2.1 accessibility gaps, particularly for assistive-technology users

## Our Approach

**Research & discovery** — Contextual interviews with business clients and treasury managers, plus competitive benchmarking of banking dashboards (JP Morgan, HSBC, Citi).

**Design strategy** — Redefined the information hierarchy to surface high-value data above the fold; created journey maps and service blueprints linking the dashboard to downstream tasks; designed responsive, modular card layouts.

**Implementation** — Customizable widgets for role-based data prioritization, full WCAG 2.1 AA compliance (contrast, ARIA labelling, keyboard navigation), and a reusable component library for the enterprise design system.

## Outcomes

- **30%** reduction in task-completion time
- Higher daily engagement with reduced navigation depth
- Full WCAG 2.1 AA certification
- A component library adopted across other RBC Express modules
- Improved client satisfaction and digital-adoption metrics
