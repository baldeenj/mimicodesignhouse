*Role: Senior Information Architect & UX Lead — Canada Post Corporation.*

## Overview

Canada Post modernized its fragmented mobile ecosystem into a unified app experience across iOS, Android, and Windows. The redesign let customers track parcels, locate post offices, manage deliveries, and access e-commerce tools from one consistent, accessible interface.

## The Challenge

- Designing responsive, touch-optimized experiences across multiple device form factors
- Keeping visual design and feature availability consistent across iOS, Android, and Windows
- Meeting WCAG accessibility compliance and government digital standards
- Balancing complex logistics data with consumer usability and brand presence

## Our Approach

**Discovery** — Stakeholder interviews, user-analytics review, and competitive benchmarking against other delivery apps to pinpoint pain points.

**Architecture** — Clear navigation structures and task-focused user flows, wireframed and prototyped in Axure and Sketch.

**Design** — Applied Modern UI principles — clean typography, live tiles, and gesture-based interactions — with multi-panel layouts for tablet workflows.

**Validation** — Usability testing across age groups and accessibility needs, with WCAG 2.0 AA standards applied throughout.

## Outcomes

- Launched a fully redesigned cross-platform app with markedly improved usability
- Increased customer satisfaction and app-store ratings
- Reduced time-to-track and post-office location by over **30%**
- Established a model for accessible, platform-consistent design
