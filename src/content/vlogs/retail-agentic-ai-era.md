## Show Notes

A 30-minute discussion between two practitioners examining agentic AI in retail operations. Separates hype from reality by distinguishing between what's in production today and what true agentic systems will actually require.

**Key distinctions covered:**

- **Autonomous Workflow Orchestration** — current ML/LLM-powered workflows already in production
- **True agentic ecosystems** — goal-driven digital coworkers with perception, reasoning, and cross-system coordination
- What gets mislabelled as "agent" technology and why it matters

**Three core topics:**

1. What's currently live in retail production
2. What gets misbranded as "agent" technology
3. Architectural changes needed to transition from orchestrated automation to agent-driven retail operations

**Target audience:** CIOs, COOs, and digital leaders seeking clarity on agentic AI implementation timelines and investment priorities.
