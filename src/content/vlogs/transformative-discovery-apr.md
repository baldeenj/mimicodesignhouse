## Show Notes

Most discovery processes are designed to gather requirements quickly. But speed doesn't guarantee understanding. This episode examines how integrating coaching techniques into discovery prevents the most common cause of project failure: building the wrong thing.

**Topics covered:**

- Shortcomings in traditional discovery approaches
- The dangers of rushing to solutions prematurely
- How coaching enriches alignment and surfaces real insights
- The importance of listening and silence as discovery tools
- Why better discovery practices yield superior outcomes — consistently

**Who this is for:** Product leaders, designers, researchers, consultants, delivery teams, and stakeholders frustrated by misaligned requirements.
