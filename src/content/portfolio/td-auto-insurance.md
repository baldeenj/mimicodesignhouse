*Role: Senior / Lead User Experience Designer — TD Insurance.*

## Overview

TD Insurance set out to modernize its web-based Auto Insurance Smart Quoter. The redesign simplified the quoting journey while ensuring full accessibility compliance and regulatory adherence — reducing friction, boosting conversion, and aligning the experience with modern design standards.

## The Challenge

- Dense forms and unclear language that confused users
- Accessibility gaps failing WCAG and AODA standards
- Multiple screens with redundant inputs complicating the journey
- Inadequate mobile optimization
- Design inconsistent with TD's evolving system

## Our Approach

**Discovery** — Usability testing and abandonment-data analysis to pinpoint friction.

**Service blueprinting** — Mapped user actions, system triggers, and back-end dependencies to eliminate redundant inputs.

**Accessibility** — WCAG 2.1 AA embedded at every stage: contrast, keyboard navigation, and screen-reader support.

**Interaction design** — Progressive-disclosure flows, simplified forms, and responsive prototypes aligned to TD's design system.

**Delivery** — Managed through Agile sprints with documentation ready for compliance audit.

## Outcomes

- Full WCAG compliance achieved
- Quote-completion time reduced by **35%**
- Quote-to-application conversion up **20%**
- Support inquiries down **25%**
- An accessible component library adopted across other TD products
