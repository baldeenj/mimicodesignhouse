*Role: Senior Information Architect & UX Lead (Windows app) — Canada Post Corporation.*

## Overview

As part of modernizing Canada Post's fragmented mobile ecosystem across iOS, Android, and Windows, this project delivered the **Windows** app — letting users track parcels, locate post offices, manage deliveries, and access e-commerce tools in a touch-optimized, platform-consistent experience.

## The Challenge

- Designing for multiple device form factors with a consistent experience
- Building touch-optimized interfaces compliant with Windows UI guidelines
- Meeting WCAG accessibility and government digital standards
- Balancing complex logistics data with consumer usability

## Our Approach

- Stakeholder interviews and competitive benchmarking
- A clear navigation hierarchy optimized for parcel tracking and management
- Wireframes and multi-panel layouts supporting parallel workflows
- Microsoft Modern UI principles with real-time tracking visuals
- WCAG 2.0 AA standards, validated through usability testing

## Outcomes

- Launched a redesigned Windows app with improved cross-platform consistency
- Higher user-satisfaction scores and app-store ratings
- Reduced time-to-track parcels by over **30%** through simplified task flows
- Established a model for accessible, platform-consistent design
