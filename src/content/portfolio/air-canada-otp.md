## Overview

An AI-powered operational dashboard designed to help Air Canada's Operations team monitor and enhance flight punctuality across their global network. The platform translates predictive machine-learning models into actionable insights for dispatchers, maintenance leads, and airport operations staff.

## The Challenge

Three interconnected obstacles required simultaneous resolution:

- Converting complex ML outputs into understandable formats for non-technical operational staff
- Designing workflows suited to time-sensitive environments where every second matters
- Establishing user confidence in AI-driven recommendations across hundreds of interdependent factors (weather, crew scheduling, gate logistics)

  <img src="/uploads/ac-otp-2.png" alt="Month-to-date on-time performance summary view." />

## Approach

- Conducted field research with operational staff in live environments
- Partnered with data scientists to understand ML capabilities and limitations
- Applied human-centered design methodology throughout
- Built usability testing into every iteration cycle
- Designed a transparency framework emphasizing confidence levels and contributing factors
- Achieved WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility compliance for operations center displays
- Developed a reusable pattern library for subsequent AI initiatives

## Outcomes

- Delivered Air Canada's **first predictive operations dashboard** with real-time network visibility
- Reduced delay-response time by **20–25%** through proactive interventions
- Enhanced user trust via transparent confidence indicators and contextual explanations
- Full accessibility compliance for operations center displays
- Reusable pattern library accelerating subsequent AI product development
