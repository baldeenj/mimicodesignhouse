## Show Notes

Broken UX rarely announces itself. It shows up as churn, support tickets, low adoption, and team misalignment. This episode addresses how to diagnose and fix product experiences that aren't working.

**Topics covered:**

- How to recognise signs that your product UX is failing before it becomes a crisis
- Connecting user research to design work in a meaningful way
- Creating intuitive, usable products grounded in authentic user research
- How to align your team around the user problem rather than the feature list
- Accelerating development with greater assurance by reducing UX debt early
