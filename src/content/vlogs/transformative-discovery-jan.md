## Show Notes

How coaching principles enhance project discovery and collaboration. The most meaningful insights don't emerge from perfectly structured questions but through deep listening and reflection.

**Topics covered:**

- Why "the most meaningful insights don't come from perfectly structured questions"
- How coaching creates psychological safety to explore problems before committing to solutions
- True collaboration doesn't come from having the best answer in the room
- Creating conditions where people can think more clearly together
- How to distinguish a problem statement from a solution statement
- Why stakeholders often skip straight to solutions — and how to bring them back
