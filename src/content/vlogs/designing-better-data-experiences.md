## Show Notes

Data teams spend enormous effort on data quality and infrastructure, then hand off dashboards that don't actually help people decide. This episode addresses why and what to do about it.

**Topics covered:**

- Why most dashboards underperform despite good underlying data
- "Most dashboards don't fail because of bad data, they fail because the design doesn't support how humans make decisions"
- How to integrate design into data workflows earlier in the process
- What decision-support tools look like vs. data output tools
- Ensuring analytics highlight signals, clarify context, and suggest actionable next steps
- The difference between showing data and supporting decisions
