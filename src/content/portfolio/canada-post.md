## The Challenge

Parcel tracking was generating significant customer service contacts — not because deliveries were failing, but because the tracking experience wasn't reducing uncertainty at the moments it mattered most.

## Our Approach

We mapped the emotional journey of waiting for a parcel, identifying the specific moments where uncertainty peaks and the information that would actually resolve it. The design challenge was surfacing the right information at the right time, not just displaying all available data.

## Outcomes

- Reduction in "where is my parcel" contacts during tracked deliveries
- Improved customer satisfaction scores for the delivery experience
- Increased engagement with proactive delivery notifications
