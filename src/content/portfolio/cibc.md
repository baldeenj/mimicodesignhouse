## Overview

A modernization initiative to redesign CIBC's digital banking account comparison and application experiences. The transformation turned static, text-heavy interfaces into responsive, interactive tools that guide customers through financial decision-making.

## The Challenge

The existing comparison system suffered from poor mobile responsiveness, unclear language laden with jargon, inconsistent design patterns, and high abandonment during the application process. Users struggled to evaluate accounts effectively or understand which product suited their needs — at exactly the moment a clear decision was most important.

<figure>
  <img src="/uploads/cibc-2.png" alt="Interactive account-comparison table filterable by customer type, with plain-language features." />

## Approach

- Conducted user research and heuristic audits to identify friction points
- Created customer journey maps spanning research through onboarding
- Designed interactive comparison tables with filtering capabilities
- Simplified financial terminology through expandable educational modules
- Built responsive prototypes in Figma and Axure for testing
- Performed usability testing on both mobile and desktop platforms
- Implemented WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards throughout

## Outcomes

- **35%** reduction in account selection time
- **20%** increase in conversion to account opening
- Full WCAG 2.1 AA certification achieved
- New comparison pattern established as the template for future CIBC products (credit cards, savings accounts)

## Tools & Disciplines

User Research · Interaction Design · Prototyping (Figma, Axure) · Journey Mapping · Usability Testing · Accessibility Compliance · Service Design
