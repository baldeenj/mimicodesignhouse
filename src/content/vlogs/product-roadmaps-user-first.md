## Show Notes

A practical framework for product managers, UX designers, founders, and engineering leads who want roadmaps that reflect real user needs — not just stakeholder wish lists.

**The six-step framework:**

1. **Define objectives** that align business and user needs
2. **Deep user research** through interviews, surveys, and observation
3. **Map ideal future-state journeys** to visualise the destination
4. **Apply RICE prioritisation** (Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort)
5. **Use Now/Next/Later timelines** instead of traditional Gantt charts
6. **Iterate with stakeholders** through structured review cycles

**Why it matters:** Most roadmaps fail not because of bad ideas but because they're disconnected from what users actually need. This framework reconnects the two.
