## Overview

Integration of Interac e-Transfer Autodeposit functionality into RBC Express, enabling business clients to receive electronic payments instantly and securely without manual intervention. The solution had to balance robust security, auditability, and clarity — critical requirements in high-value business transactions.

## The Challenge

Business banking clients needed a streamlined experience that simultaneously satisfied multiple competing demands: security and auditability, complex approval hierarchies across midsize to large organizations, and user confidence in automated deposits for high-value transactions.

## Approach

- Conducted user research mapping payment workflows and pain points with business banking clients
- Partnered with RBC technical and business teams to define system requirements around permissions and notifications
- Developed wireframes, prototypes, and UI components for registration and management workflows
- Tested interactive prototypes iteratively with stakeholders and real users
- Created documentation and training materials for onboarding support

## Outcomes

- Enabled businesses to receive payments directly with automated reconciliation
- Increased user adoption of the Autodeposit feature
- Reduced administrative burden and payment error rates
- Strengthened RBC's competitive position through client-centric features
- Maintained the highest security and compliance standards throughout
