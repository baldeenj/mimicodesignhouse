## Show Notes

Baha and Donnafay discuss how coaching techniques transform discovery processes, drawing on real examples from design and retail implementation to demonstrate how better conversations lead to understanding actual needs rather than just requested features.

**What we cover:**

- Why "thoughtful questions, silence, and curiosity help people find clarity they didn't realize they already had"
- How to use silence as a discovery tool
- The difference between a problem statement and a solution statement
- Why stakeholders often skip to the latter — and how to bring them back
- Real examples from fintech and retail engagements where the stated request masked the real problem
- How coaching transforms team communication and builds shared understanding
